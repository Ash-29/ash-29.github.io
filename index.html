About

I am a Pre-Doctoral Researcher at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), working on multimodal learning, robust vision systems, and transformer-based perception. My research lies in representation learning, vision–language modeling, diffusion-based reconstruction, and Sim2Real generalization for challenging visual environments.

Previously, I worked with researchers at Carnegie Mellon University, University of Central Florida, National University of Singapore, and Cohere for AI, contributing to research in VLMs, biomedical AI, VLA, and geospatial modeling.
